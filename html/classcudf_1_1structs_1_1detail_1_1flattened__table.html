详细描述

Result of flatten_nested_columns(), where all STRUCT columns are replaced with their non-nested member columns, and BOOL8 columns for their null masks.

flatten_nested_columns() produces a "flattened" table_view with all STRUCT columns replaced with their child column_views, preceded by their null masks. All newly allocated columns and device_buffers that back the returned table_view are also encapsulated in flatten_result.

Objects of flatten_result need to kept alive while its table_view is accessed.
