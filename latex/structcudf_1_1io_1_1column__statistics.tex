Contains per-\/column ORC statistics. 

All columns can have the {\ttfamily number\+\_\+of\+\_\+values} statistics. Depending on the data type, a column can have additional statistics, accessible through {\ttfamily type\+\_\+specific\+\_\+stats} accessor.
