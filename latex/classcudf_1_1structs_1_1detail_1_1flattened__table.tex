\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Result of {\ttfamily \doxylink{namespacecudf_1_1structs_1_1detail_a09c8725783cc2d0c3ce2f537a5ef92fb}{flatten\+\_\+nested\+\_\+columns()}}, where all {\ttfamily STRUCT} columns are replaced with their non-\/nested member columns, and {\ttfamily BOOL8} columns for their null masks. 

{\ttfamily \doxylink{namespacecudf_1_1structs_1_1detail_a09c8725783cc2d0c3ce2f537a5ef92fb}{flatten\+\_\+nested\+\_\+columns()}} produces a \"{}flattened\"{} \doxylink{classcudf_1_1table__view}{table\+\_\+view} with all {\ttfamily STRUCT} columns replaced with their child column\+\_\+views, preceded by their null masks. All newly allocated columns and device\+\_\+buffers that back the returned \doxylink{classcudf_1_1table__view}{table\+\_\+view} are also encapsulated in {\ttfamily flatten\+\_\+result}.

Objects of {\ttfamily flatten\+\_\+result} need to kept alive while its \doxylink{classcudf_1_1table__view}{table\+\_\+view} is accessed.
