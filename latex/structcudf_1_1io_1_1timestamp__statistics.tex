\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Statistics for timestamp columns. 

The {\ttfamily minimum} and {\ttfamily maximum} min/max elements in the column, as the number of milliseconds since the UNIX epoch. The {\ttfamily minimum\+\_\+utc} and {\ttfamily maximum\+\_\+utc} are the same values adjusted to UTC.
