Statistics for string columns. 

The {\ttfamily minimum} and {\ttfamily maximum} are the first and last elements, respectively, in lexicographical order. The {\ttfamily sum} is the total length of elements in the column. Note\+: According to ORC specs, the sum should be signed, but pyarrow uses unsigned value
