Constants to disambiguate statistics terminology for ORC. 

ORC refers to its finest granularity of row-\/grouping as \"{}row group\"{}, which corresponds to Parquet \"{}pages\"{}. Similarly, ORC\textquotesingle{}s \"{}stripe\"{} corresponds to a Parquet \"{}row group\"{}. The following constants disambiguate the terminology for the statistics collected at each level.
